UI/UX Designer

UI UX designers create the user interface for an app, website, or other interactive media. Their work includes collaborating with product managers and engineers to gather requirements from users before designing ideas that can be communicated using storyboards. They also process flows or sitemaps.
As someone who enjoys designing things and making things simple, I was attracted to this career scope as it involves making UI/UX designs that cater to others, both aesthetically and effectively.

Game Developer

Game developers are the people who take all of the assets produced by other members of the game development team, program them in the game engine, and bring the game to life in a technical sense.
As someone who was interested in how games worked since young, I always had a dream on making my own when I was older. Being able to program a game or be part of a development team will be a dream come true.

Web Developer

A Web Developer is a professional who is responsible for the design and construction of websites. They ensure that sites meet user expectations by ensuring they look good, run smoothly and offer easy access points with no loading issues between pages or error messages. I had enjoyed making this website, both from the wireframe and coding out the HTML and CSS. I liked to see the elements appear on the screen where I wanted them to, and the feeling I get when I see how my CSS codes work fine. Hence, I actually have a big interest in this.
